ttery Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

ttery is designed to work on your device. We do not operate user accounts, advertising, analytics, cloud sync, or a server for your task data.

Information stored by ttery

ttery stores the tasks you create or edit, your task selections, energy state, reminder preferences, and related app settings on your device. This information is used only to provide the app’s planning, energy, reminder, and widget features.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, ttery schedules local notifications through Apple’s notification services. The app does not send your task information to a ttery server. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in your device settings.

Home Screen widget

The optional ttery widget reads the energy state, active task, and selected-task count shared locally between the app and its widget extension. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Information we do not collect

In the current version, ttery does not collect or transmit names, email addresses, contacts, location, health data, payment information, browsing history, identifiers, usage analytics, or advertising data. ttery does not track you across apps or websites.

Third-party services

The current version does not include third-party analytics, advertising, account, or cloud-storage services. If this changes, we will update this policy before or when the relevant feature is introduced.

Data deletion

You can delete individual tasks inside ttery. Deleting the app removes its locally stored app data according to the behavior of your device and operating system. Because the current version does not store data on our servers, we do not hold a separate server copy to delete.

Children’s privacy

ttery does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. Since the current version does not transmit personal information, there is no account or registration process.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when ttery’s features or data practices change. The effective date at the top of this page will indicate when the latest version was published.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app’s publisher through the ttery project contact page.
